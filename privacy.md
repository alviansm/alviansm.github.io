This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you visit this website.

## Data Collection

This website is a static site and does not actively collect, store, or process any personal data from its visitors.

- **No Cookies:** We do not use cookies to track you.
- **No Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics) that track your browsing behavior.
- **No User Accounts:** You cannot create an account, log in, or submit personal information through forms on this site.

## Server Logs

Like most standard websites, the server infrastructure hosting this site (e.g., GitHub Pages) may automatically collect standard log data. This data is collected by the hosting provider and may (or may not) include:

- The IP address of the device requesting the page
- The type of browser and operating system used
- The time and date of access
- The referring website

This data is typically used by the hosting provider for security, error tracking, and maintaining server performance. It is not used by the site owner to identify individual visitors.

## Links to External Sites

This website may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.
